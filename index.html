my history

At the end of high school, I didn’t know which major I wanted to pursue. My parents didn’t have enough money to support me in another city, so I applied to a small federal university in my hometown. I had never had any contact with programming or the IT world before. At the beginning, I struggled a lot, but I kept pushing myself to improve, understand more about technology, and never give up. Sometimes I feel like I’m behind, but as I like to say: "Since I'm looking from behind, I know the way to go forward." Over time, it has been incredibly rewarding to realize what I’m capable of doing, especially in a field I once thought I would never understand. I may not be exactly where I want to be yet, but I trust that one day I’ll look back and feel proud of how far I’ve come.
